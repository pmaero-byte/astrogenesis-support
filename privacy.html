Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

AstroGenesis is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

To provide accurate astrological calculations, AstroGenesis collects the following information:

  • Birth Date
  • Birth Time
  • Birth Location (City or Coordinates)

What We Do NOT Collect

We value your anonymity. AstroGenesis does NOT collect:

  • Real names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Photos or media
  • Health data

Data Usage and Processing

Your birth data is sent to our secure API server (astrogenesis backend) solely for the purpose of calculating your astrological charts. We do not use third-party analytics, we do not show ads, and we do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

Data Storage

Your birth profiles are stored locally on your device. We do not require account registration, so your data remains under your control on your own hardware.

Location Services

AstroGenesis may request access to your location while using the app to help you easily set up your birth chart based on your current position. This is optional and can be disabled in your device settings.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website at astrogenesis.net.
